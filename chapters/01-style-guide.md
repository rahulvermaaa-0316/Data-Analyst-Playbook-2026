# 📘 Data Analyst Playbook 2026

# Style Guide

## Writing Style

- Use simple English.
- Explain every topic with real-world examples.
- Avoid unnecessary theory.
- Focus on interview preparation.

---

## Chapter Structure

Every chapter must follow this order:

1. Learning Objectives
2. Introduction
3. Why It Matters
4. Theory
5. Real Business Example
6. Practical Example
7. Interview Questions
8. Practice Questions
9. Common Mistakes
10. Cheat Sheet
11. Chapter Summary

---

## Code Style

- Use clean formatting.
- Add comments wherever required.
- Explain every SQL query.
- Explain every Python code example.

---

## Business Examples

Every concept should include at least one real-world business example.

---

## Interview Tips

Include interview tips wherever applicable.

---

## Practice Questions

Each chapter should contain:

- Easy Questions
- Medium Questions
- Hard Questions

---

## Cheat Sheet

End every chapter with a one-page quick revision.

---

## Goal

This playbook is designed to help students prepare for internships and entry-level Data Analyst jobs.
